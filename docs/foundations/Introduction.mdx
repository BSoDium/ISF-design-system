# ISF Design System

The **Ingénieurs sans Frontières** design system provides a cohesive visual language for all ISF digital products. It draws on an editorial, institutional aesthetic — blending the clarity of France's **Système de Design de l'État**, the informational density of **Wikipedia**, and the typographic sophistication of **The New York Times**.

---

## Design Principles

### Clarity
Every element serves a purpose. The interface should feel immediately legible, with a clear hierarchy that guides the reader through content without friction.

### Accessibility
Built on Mantine's `autoContrast` and WCAG-compliant color pairings, the system ensures readability across all color schemes and device contexts. Dark mode is a first-class citizen, not an afterthought.

### Editorial Sophistication
ISF communicates expertise and thought leadership. The typography — **Playfair Display** for headings paired with **Geist** for body text — evokes quality journalism and academic publishing, lending authority to every page.

### Institutional Trust
As an engineering NGO, ISF must project reliability and professionalism. The deep teal and ivory palette, restrained use of copper accents, and subtle shadows create a sense of gravitas without austerity.

---

## Visual Identity at a Glance

### Color Palette
A warm, grounded palette anchored by **Deep Ocean** teal (`#154C56`) and **Ivory Sand** (`#FFF4E5`). **Copper** (`#B95118`) provides energetic accents for calls to action, while **Midnight** (`#1F2D33`) and **Blue Ash** (`#5A7381`) handle text and secondary surfaces. The palette supports full dark mode through virtual color tokens that invert surfaces and foregrounds seamlessly.

### Typography
- **Headings**: Playfair Display — a refined serif with editorial character, used for all heading levels (h1–h6).
- **Body**: Geist — a clean, modern sans-serif optimised for screen readability at all sizes.
- The pairing creates a strong visual hierarchy that feels both contemporary and timeless.

### Spacing & Layout
A five-step spacing scale (xs–xl) ensures consistent rhythm across components and page layouts. The editorial aesthetic favors generous whitespace to let content breathe.

### Shadows & Elevation
Subtle, desaturated shadows (tinted with Midnight) provide a paper-like depth. Five levels from `xs` (barely-there) to `xl` (prominent card lift) allow for nuanced layering without visual noise.

### Border Radius
Sharp by default (`sm` = 0.25rem), with options up to `xl` (1rem) for softer UI elements. The editorial direction favors crisp edges — rounded corners are used sparingly and intentionally.
